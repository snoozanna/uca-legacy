    About

Welcome to the 1884FM Experience, an interactive digital experience exploring the history of the 1884 Berlin Conference.

It was created by Rhianna Ilube & Coney, as part of their show .

You can play a series of games to debunk myths about 1884 and discover the truth behind them.

You can visit the 1884 memorial to read or listen to reflections about 1884 and record your own.

Or you can access historical resources, articles and podcasts.

You'll be able to visit any of these pages at any time by clicking on the menu icon in the top right corner.
