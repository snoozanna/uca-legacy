Episode 2

The Secret of Trees

The Secret of Trees focuses on teamwork, emphasising the importance of trees and collective action.

In this second instalment of UCA, pupils receive a mysterious box from Anne, a member of UCA, to investigate mysterious signals surrounding their school. Through curriculum-based exercises covering tree anatomy, photosynthesis, and letter writing, students learn about climate activists Wangari Maathai & Felix Finkbeiner’s, the vital role of tree planting in climate crisis mitigation, and the importance of local action.
