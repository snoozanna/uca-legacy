The Static

The Static is an interactive adventure for Year 3 or Year 4 to explore mental health and wellbeing.

In this episode UCA explores The Static, a mysterious presence that stops people feeling able to connect with one another, and isolates those with their own worries. The Static is a playful way to describe anxiety.

You’ll receive an UCA Beacon, which when lit, helps combat the static.

Students are tasked with helping Jay, a newly trained member of the Undercover Caretaker Agency, who is concerned about their friend and colleague at the agency Mal, who seems to be isolating themselves from the rest of the team. Through exercises based on the 5 Ways To Wellbeing , students are upskilled in methods to look after their own mental health and support their peers to do the same. The adventure culminates in helping them share what they learn with the rest of their school through creating a creative project to benefit the wellbeing of their community.

Episode 1's Tutorial takes around 20-25 minutes. We recommend running the tutorial with the box and the teacher pack with you.
